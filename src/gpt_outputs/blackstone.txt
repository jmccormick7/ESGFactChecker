Blackstone Group Inc. is an American multinational private equity firm, alternative asset management, and financial services corporation. It is one of the largest investment firms in the world. Blackstone specializes in private equity, credit, and hedge fund investment strategies. It was founded in 1985 by Peter G. Peterson and Stephen A. Schwarzman. The firm manages investments across multiple asset classes, including real estate, infrastructure, energy, and private equity funds. Blackstone is headquartered in New York City and has offices in various cities worldwide.
