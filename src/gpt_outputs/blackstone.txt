- Blackstone has completed a $1 billion equity investment in renewable energy company Invenergy, bringing its total investment in the company to $4 billion.
- The additional investment will be used to support the growth of Invenergy's portfolio of projects, including large-scale solar and wind farms.
- Blackstone is seeking to bolster its sustainability credentials as investors increasingly focus on the environmental and social impact of companies.
- The private equity firm has also sold shares in ESG-focused company Altus Power, triggering a 25% drop in Altus's share price.
- Blackstone has acquired a majority stake in Emerson Electric's climate technologies unit in a deal valuing the business at $14 billion, as Blackstone pivots towards the automation market.
