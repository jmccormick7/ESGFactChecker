- Patagonia is a $3 billion company that is focused on sustainable practices and fighting climate change.
- The founder, Yvon Chouinard, and his family have announced that they will be giving away their wealth to fight the climate crisis.
- Patagonia has implemented measures to reduce its environmental impact, such as the Worn Wear program which keeps products in circulation and reduces waste.
- The company is invested in raising awareness about environmental issues and promoting sustainability in the business world.
- The largest continuous kelp forest in the world is located in Chile's Patagonia region, which is crucial for battling climate change and supporting biodiversity.
